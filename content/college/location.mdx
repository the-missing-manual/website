title: Location
description: Why geography still matters even with remote internships and online hackathons.
---

Colleges near **Bangalore, Hyderabad, or Pune** tend to give you more exposure: more meetups, more internship options nearby, more people building things around you outside of college.

Hackathons are increasingly online and internships are often remote, so location is less of a hard constraint than it was five years ago. <Mark>Physical proximity to a tech ecosystem still shows up in ways that are hard to quantify.</Mark> Who you are around in your off time, which events you casually attend, which professionals you end up meeting. These compound over four years.

It is not a dealbreaker if a strong college is in a smaller city. It is worth weighing if you are choosing between options that are otherwise comparable.
