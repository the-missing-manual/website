### Management Quota

Direct admission with no entrance exam. Seats are usually **15 to 20 percent** of total intake in private colleges. Fees are typically **1.5x to 3x higher** than merit seats.

<Callout type="warn">
Get the exact fee structure in writing before committing. The number in the brochure and the number in the fee schedule are not always the same.
</Callout>


### Merit-Based Admission

Goes through an entrance exam. JEE Main for most central institutions. For state-level admissions:

| State | Exam |
|-------|------|
| Karnataka (government colleges) | KCET |
| Maharashtra | MHT-CET |
| Tamil Nadu | TNEA |
| Karnataka (private colleges) | COMEDK |


### Lateral Entry

Lets you join 2nd year directly if you hold a diploma or BSc in a relevant field. Seat count is usually **10 to 20 percent** of normal intake, added as a separate pool. Not every college offers this, and availability varies by state.


### Branch Change

Officially available at some colleges if you clear 1st year with a CGPA above a set threshold, typically 8.5 but varies by institution, with no backlogs.

Branch change into CSE is rare and competitive even where it is allowed. Seats are few, demand is high, and the process is institution-specific.

<Callout type="warn">
Do not build your admission plan around getting a branch change into CSE. Treat it as a possibility, not a strategy.
</Callout>
