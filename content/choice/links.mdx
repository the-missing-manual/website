description: A curated list of resources to help you research colleges, understand the admission process, and stay updated on the latest trends in CSE education.

| Resource | Use it for |
|----------|-----------|
| [NAAC accreditation search](https://www.naac.gov.in) | Institution-level accreditation grade and when it was last issued |
| [NIRF engineering rankings](https://www.nirfindia.org/Rankings/2025/EngineeringRanking.html) | National ranking, to place a college in a rough tier |
| [NBA program accreditation](https://www.nbaind.org) | Branch-specific accreditation for the CSE program |
| [Devfolio](https://devfolio.co) | Searching a college's hackathon teams and history |
| [Unstop](https://unstop.com) | Competitions and challenges |
| [AmbitionBox](https://www.ambitionbox.com) | Verifying company salaries against reported CTC |
| [GSoC archive](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/archive) | Alumni search by organization |
